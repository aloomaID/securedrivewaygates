Lake Holm, WA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Lake Holm is located in southwestern King County at 47°18′23″N 122°07′48″W (47.306388, -122.129911). It is bordered to the west by the city of Auburn and to the north by the Lake Morton-Berrydale CDP. The southern border of the Lake Holm CDP follows the Green River, and the northern border follows Big Soos Creek and Covington Creek, tributaries of the Green. Lake Holm is 19 miles (31 km) east of Tacoma and 27 miles (43 km) south-southeast of Seattle.
